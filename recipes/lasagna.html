Monster Lasagna

What... in this?:

A (tipically) meaty delicatessen cooked in a crock pot by combining 2 monster food items, which include monster meat, monster jerky, durians (wtf?) and jellyfish. It takes 10 seconds to cook. Talk about flash-heated products.

'Tastes like hairs and meats with noodle.'

Ingredients:

The monster lasagna can be made in a bunch of different ways.

  • The Faithful

    • Monster meat x4

  • The Seasoned

    • Monster meat x2
    • Berry x2

  • The Extra Proteined

    (May result in Bacon and Eggs if not cooked properly)

    • Monster meat x2
    • Egg x2

  • The Balanced

    (May result in a shrieking Mandrake Soup)

    • Monster meat x1
    • Durian x1
    • Mandrake x1

  • The Senseless

    (Defiying all common sense. Monsterless monster lasagna)

    • Durian x2
    • Berry x2

Preparation:

In order to make any of the aforementioned versions of the dish, one must follow the following steps carefully:

  1. Turn on your good ol' Crock Pot.
  2. Deposit all of the needed ingredients inside.
  3. Wait 10 seconds. (This short time is of good use to pray for better kitchen equipment and, more importantly, better, less aggressive ingredients.)
  4. Enjoy your freshly cooked meal warm!

Disclaimer: Monster Lasagna consumption is linked to overall drop of both physical and psychological wellbeing. Studies have found the quantifiable health impact to be exactly -20 on both body and psyche.
